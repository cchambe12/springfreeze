\section*{Glossary}

\textbf{Budburst: } the timing when buds exit the dormany phase and the first green tips are visible 

\textbf{Leafout: } the timing when leaves are fully unfolded following budburst 

\textbf{False Spring: } damaging freezing temperatures after budburst and before full leaf expansion 

\textbf{Duration of Vegetative Risk} the number of days between budburst and leafout 

\textbf{Avoidance Strategies: } flexible spring phenolgies where plants with later budburst times or shorter durations of vegetative risk have a lower risk of exposure to false springs 

\textbf{Tolerance Strategies: } morphological differences that reduce the risk, or extent of, ice formation during a false spring 

\textbf{Phenological Cues: } the three major cues (i.e. winter chilling temperatures, spring warming temperatures, and daylength) and their interactions that influence budburst and leafout
